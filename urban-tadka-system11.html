Our Philosophy

Where Heritage
Meets Haute Cuisine

Urban Tadka was born from a singular obsession: to honour the profound complexity of India's culinary heritage while pushing it into uncharted, contemporary territory.

Our kitchen sources single-origin spices from heirloom farms, ages proteins in Himalayan salt chambers, and draws on techniques from Paris, Tokyo, and your grandmother's kitchen — in equal measure.
